Cultural route

In this cultural route you will come across different museums & cultural places of Amsterdam. This route will begin at the Central Station of Amsterdam and from there you will start your bike adventure. After Central Station you can bike to the Dam Palace which is used by the royal Dutch house. From here you can bike to Foam which is an photography museum. When you are finished overhere you could check out Paradiso & Melkweg where you can visit concert or grab a party. And then we are almost done with this route as we come close to all of most popular museums of Amsterdam. Such like Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum & Stedelijk museum.
