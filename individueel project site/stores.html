Japanese stores

In Amsterdam we also have some Japanese stores. So if you would like to be back in Japan for a minute this your route! You can start at Henk Comics where they sell anime collectorsitems and manga's. This store is in Zeedijk so you will notice a lot of asians restaurants here. You could also visit Oriental or Shilla for some ramen to make them at home.
At the American Book Store they also have some mangas. Inside this shop they also a cafe so you can order something to drink while you are reading one of your favourite manga's. Closeby you have 't Japanse Winkeltje (The small Japanse store) where you can a lot of different items. Such like kimono's, Japanese tea and more. So don't hesitate to visit it!
