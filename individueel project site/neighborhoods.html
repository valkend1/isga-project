Neighborhoods

In this route you will go through different neighborhoods of Amsterdam. This route starts from Central Station and from here you can bike or walk to Chinatown (Zeedijk) of Amsterdam. Close by Zeedijk you will come across one of the most popular neighborhoods of Amsterdam: Red Light District. But be careful overhere when you are biking as it is more of a walking neighborhood!
From here you can go to 9 Straatjes (9 Streets) where you have cute little stores for clothing and more. When you are done exploring this streets you can go to the last stop of this route: De Pijp. Overhere you can find some nice restaurants/bars where you could end your day trip.
