Japanese activities

Amsterdam has some activities where you could get reminded of the activities that you have in Japan. Such like Otaking where you can get your favourite anime figure from a Gashapon. This is also the only Gashapon shop in Europe. Next place that we have is Duke of Tokyo where you can do some karaoke. This place is decorated like a real karaoke place of Japan and the cocktails are really nice. If you have some nice weather then you should go to Amstelpark and go through the Japanese Garden. And of course we can't forget to play some games at an arcade hall. In Amsterdam we have a couple which are small, but in Amsterdam South-East there is a bigger one named Gamestate.
