Japanese Restaurants

If you love Japanese food, then is the route for you! There are a lot of different places so we have a route with a couple of restaurants that you can visit. The first one on the route is called Ramen Kingdom which is just a couple of minutes away from Central Station. Here you can get authentic Japanese Ramen.
Closeby the neighborhood De Pijp there is a market called Albert Cuyp. Here you can find Osakabal where their signature dishes are Takoyaki & Okonomiyaki. So fresh and delicious! And just around the corner you will also find De Jappanner (The Japanese Man) for some Japanese figer food like sushi.
And last but not least we also have Hakata Senpachi which is close to Rai. They are famous for the Yakitori which are grilled on their inside bbq. So much options to choose from, but we can assure it that everything will be delicious!
