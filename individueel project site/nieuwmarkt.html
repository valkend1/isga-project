Nieuwmarkt

Nieuwmarkt is a great meeting spot. Around this square you will find cafes and bars where you can eat and drink something. Closeby you also have Zeedijk which is the Chinatown of Amsterdam. And behind Zeedijk you will find Red District, one of the most famous neighborhoods of Amsterdam. So if you would like to explore different parts of the city, start from Nieuwmarkt.
