Shopping route

In the center of Amsterdam there are some shopping streets. You can bike to them but it may be easier to walk through these streets. You can start from Central Station and from here you can go to Nieuwendijk or Damrak. Both of these streets have different types of stores. At Nieuwendijk there is a Hema where you can buy the popular Stroopwafels, cookie with some caramel filling. If you get to the end of one of this streets you will arrive at Dam Square, where you will see the big Dam Palace. If you want to continue shopping you can go to Kalverstraat, which is another street with all kinds of stores.
